# When to Choose What (Decision Framework)

## Choose Fabric when:
- Microsoft-first org (Power BI is strategic)
- You need rapid BI delivery + unified experience
- You want lakehouse + BI without tool sprawl

## Choose Databricks when:
- Heavy transformation / engineering workflows
- Spark-native scale and flexibility matters
- You need deeper ML/feature engineering capability

## Choose Snowflake when:
- SQL-first analytics and warehouse serving are primary
- Workload isolation is important
- You want strong, clean warehouse operating model

## Choose Hybrid when:
- Engineering teams own transformation in Databricks
- Analytics teams want curated serving in Snowflake
- BI teams run consumption in Fabric/Power BI
- You need clear platform boundaries and shared governance
